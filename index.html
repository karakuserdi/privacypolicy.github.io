Last Updated: November 15, 2025

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account or use the App, we may collect your email address and other registration details.
  • Input Content: Text prompts, photos, and videos you upload to generate AI-powered images and videos.
  • User Content: All content generated through the App, including AI-generated images and videos.
  • Communication Data: When you contact us for support, we collect your email and message content.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, time spent on the App, interaction patterns, performance data, and generation count (number of images/videos created).
  • App Activity: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information.

1.3 Generation Limits

To ensure fair usage and maintain service quality for all users, we track the number of AI-generated images and videos you create. All users are subject to a monthly generation limit of 500 images/videos, regardless of subscription status. This limit resets on the first day of each calendar month.

1.4 Payment and Subscription Information

  • Payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store. We do not store your credit card or payment information.
  • We receive transaction confirmations and subscription status from the App Store.

2. PHOTO DATA & AI PROCESSING

2.1 How We Process Your Photos

When you upload photos to the App for AI transformation:

  • Photos are securely transmitted to our processing servers via encrypted connections.
  • Photos are processed by our AI models through Replicate's Gemini API to generate artistic transformations.
  • Automatic Deletion: All uploaded photos are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 24 hours of upload.
  • Generated images (Output Content) remain accessible to you until you delete them or terminate your account.
  • Usage Limits: All users, regardless of subscription status, have a monthly limit of 500 image generations to ensure fair usage and system stability.

2.2 Face Data & Image Processing

IMPORTANT: We do not collect, analyze, or store facial data or biometric identifiers.

All photos, including those that may contain faces, are processed solely to apply visual effects and AI-generated transformations (e.g., anime, cartoon, portrait, or fantasy styles).

  • No Facial Recognition: The App does not perform facial recognition, face mapping, or identity analysis.
  • No Biometric Data Collection: We do not create, infer, or retain any data that can identify or authenticate an individual based on facial features.
  • Artistic Rendering Only: The App's AI models process images exclusively for artistic rendering purposes and do not perform biometric analysis of any kind.
  • Temporary Processing: Photos are processed temporarily on secure servers and automatically deleted within 24 hours.
  • No Database Storage: We do not maintain any database of facial features, face templates, or biometric identifiers.
  • Third-Party Processing: Photos are processed through Replicate's Gemini AI model, which operates under strict data processing agreements and does not retain your images after processing.

What We Do:

  • Apply artistic filters and style transfers to your photos
  • Generate creative transformations (anime, cartoon, fantasy, portraits, avatars, etc.)
  • Enhance images using AI algorithms
  • Process images to deliver the visual effects you request

What We DO NOT Do:

  • Identify individuals from facial features
  • Authenticate users based on face data
  • Create biometric profiles or templates
  • Match faces across different photos
  • Analyze demographic characteristics from faces
  • Store or retain any facial recognition data
  • Share face data with third parties for identification purposes

Purpose of Processing: The App uses uploaded photos containing faces strictly for generating AI art results. No facial recognition, face mapping, or identity analysis is performed. Your photos are used only to create the artistic transformations you request.

2.3 Data Security During Processing

  • All photos are transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).
  • Photos are stored temporarily in secure, access-controlled storage during processing.
  • We implement strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access to uploaded content.
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your uploaded photos with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • No Retention: Uploaded photos are never used to train AI models or for any purpose beyond generating your requested output.
